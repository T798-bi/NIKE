Product Details

Innovative Design

The Nike Air Max Plus features an innovative design that combines stability and style, with 100% visible Nike Air cushioning. The unique overlays on the upper celebrate another iconic design from the Tn series.

Max Air Comfort

Revolutionary Max Air cushioning provides unparalleled comfort and impact absorption. Experience the feeling of walking on air with every step you take.

Flexible Fit

The synthetic upper material moves with your foot, providing a flexible fit that doesn't sacrifice support. The lacing system ensures a secure, customizable fit.

Durable Outsole

The durable rubber outsole provides traction and longevity. The modified Waffle outsole pattern offers multi-surface traction for confident strides.

Advanced Technology

The Nike Air Max Plus features advanced technology that sets it apart from other sneakers:

  • Tuned Air technology for impact protection
  • Dynamic upper support system
  • TPU overlays for stability and lockdown
  • Phylon midsole for lightweight cushioning
  • Rubber waffle outsole for durability and traction

Size & Fit

Fit: True to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
